You want to have access to the Corporate network (e.g. in your home office). Carry out the following 3 steps.
  1. Activate AlwaysOn
    Type AlwaysOn in the search box, and then click Activate AlwaysOn.
  2. Open Pulse Secure
    Open the Windows system tray, and double-click Pulse Secure to open it.
  3. Check connections
    Make sure AlwaysOn is connected. If necessary, click Connect next to AlwaysOn.
